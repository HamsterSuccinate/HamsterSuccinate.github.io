Posted on 2020-08-26 Edited on 2020-09-14 In comedy

Meet the sanctimonious family:

  • Father : San Jorge,

  • Mother: Catherine the Great

  • First kid : archangel Gabriel / archangela Gabriela

  • Second kid : angel Sophus/ angela Sophia.

Santa Claus wishes them a merry Christmas and a happy new year.

Angel Sophus / Angela Sophia,

Ho, Ho, Ho ! Merry Christmas. This is Santa here at the North Pole. Assistant elves, Noro and Rota; Associate elves, IBD and GIST; Full elves, Hepta and Pancrea are very busy getting everything together for the big day coming soon.

Santa knows that you have been a very good angel/angela this year. Thanks to the celestial rivalry you provide , the prospect of your immediate superior, archangel Gabirel / archangela Gabriela (who goes to a good school Princeton , Jale/Yale) , is as grand as her first grand prize. Of course your expectation will be ¡ más grande !

Santa also knows that you have been very good at making San Jorge and Catherine the Great proud. Thanks to you they can tell those selfish shallow and self-absorbed childfree brats to make way for them and boast about “We spent the whole night in the bathroom”

Santa has especially noticed how good you have gotten at teaching yourself Spanish. Thanks to you Catherine the Great can be peachy with joy. “ Where did he/she learn this ? “ “TB/V, Honey TV/B”

Santa sees that you have such incredible self-control that you use the bathroom all the time. Unlike the spoiled dragono/dragona deservedly slain by San Jorge , it leaves a trace of litter wherever it goes while screaming ¡me cago en todo lo que se meñea!

I will make sure to leave something special for you under the tree. I am looking forward to dropping off your gifts.

Merry Christmas!

Santa Claus, Childfree and Diversidad Sensible

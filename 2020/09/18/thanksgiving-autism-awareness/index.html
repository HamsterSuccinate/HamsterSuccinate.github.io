Out-of-season Autism Awareness

Posted on 2020-09-18 Edited on 2020-11-04 In performance art

I need your support for my out-of-season autism awareness campaign.

The campaign intends to highlight a conveniently overlooked fact : autism exists all year round even beyond the assigned autism awareness month of April. Holiday season, due to routine interruption, inconsiderate social expectation and sometimes rude neurotypical tormenters , can be particularly difficult for us on the spectrum. Our needs are NOT met.

Now I go to the details of my campaign.

  • Audience. Residents from 7405 to 7420 Holly Ave, Takoma Park, MD . The specific location is chosen for holiday spirits. We want to be holly.

  • Means of communication. Thank-you cards are mailed to the audience. Their names are found on whitepage . In case the information being inaccurate, all cards are addressed to current residents and/or [whitepage name]. The front of the card says : Thank you for your kind expression of sympathy during this difficult time. These cards are typically used in funerals. But they are oddly appropriate in pleading sympathies.

  • Back of envelope. Mainstream perceptions of autism are handwritten for a whiff of irony. This letter promotes the following political opinions:

    • Autism is not a normal neurology. It should be labelled as illness and disability

    • The stigma and bureaucracy of illness and disability should be maintained to compel someone to suck it up

    • Sharing disagreeable political opinions can get one into a lot of trouble

    • For an academic program, it matters not whether students are helped. The only thing that matters is what we require

  • Message. Messages inside are scooped from an online survey created by a self-advocacy group based on their college experiences and reflections.

  • Timing. The cards will be mailed on Thanksgiving Nov 26th ,2020. We are grateful.

We exist. We reject some of your neurotypical social norms because they are toxic. We do not apologize.

Respect us and help prevent destructive returns of the repressed, i.e. Sandy Hook and Toronto Van Rampage.

I am raising 20 dollars to cover the expense of the cards and postage. Your support is appreciated.

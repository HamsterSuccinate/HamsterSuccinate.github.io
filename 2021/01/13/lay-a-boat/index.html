Monologue of a Mooning Senior

Posted on 2021-01-13 In comedy

I am an aimless senior of a buttock (you can also say bottom ) high school of the poorest state of the — um, not so sure, great-never-so-impossible-to-be-great-again or great-ever-does-not-need-to-be-great-again United States. Guess which state ? Tip, Lydon Johnson , War on Poverty. “Let’s send the kids from DC up there to see the poor”. Yay ! West Virginia, mountain MAMA .

Let me entertain you with my mooning. It will surely make the least accomplished DC kids look like a rising star. I know, you are welcome.

First off, I am the president of the pedestrian club. I mean, how can I just be one of the team. There is no I in team but there is definitely i in president. It absolutely matters even though it is a one-person club. What I do or we (this pronoun confers immense social justification) do is the opposite of a drama club. I mean, well, thespians distort their faces and voices fanatically to impress the audience. We reduce our facial and verbal activities to the minimum, like a sloth. It turns out everyone, especially authority figures , is impressed with my apathy. My unresponsiveness makes them want to take a swing at me. It goes without saying that nobody will write me a letter of recommendation.

Second, I am the president of the loafering team. The team is one of a kind. Again, it’s a one-person team. We saunter on-and-off trail in our almost heaven. In peace and joy we traverse the entrails of MAMA like roundworms. Health authorities deworm. I mean, what awful lack of empathy. Consider the worms !

Third, I an ambassador of Men in CHINK. CHINK stands for cooking, homemaking, ironing (and irony), nursing and knitting. Sounds terrifically progressive, doesn’t it ? As an ambassador my goal is to create more opportunities for boys to pursue CHINK at both the high school and the middle school level. Hillbilly with such a left elitist goal, I will not make any difference. Opportunities are not to be no matter how many times I cant , “Let there be opportunities ! “. A simple feeling of irrelevance. By the way China does take away our jobs and spread the virus. I am just stating facts.

Fourth, I am a member of all dishonor societies (science, Spanish, art and you name it) barring two: substance use and mountain talk. I won the last mite (this means small in mountain talk) penalty (as opposed to first grand prize) in substance use ignorance unfair (as opposed to science fair) all by myself. My family just happen to own both a pain management clinic and an addiction treatment center. And I am fluent in mountain talk through hard work not because coal miners in the hollers over yander form our client base.

Last but not least, I am interning at our addiction treatment center. It is so much fun to watch former coal miners swing back and forth between our two facilities like a pendulum after their fortunate injuries (fortunate for my family of course). Thanks to my training in CHINK I can penetrate their gnarled and knotted veins much better than any female nurse. Moreover they love my pedestrian personality. Substance is substantial in that it leaves no place for love in user’s mind.

I need to go back to my mooning. People always marvel at rising star preppies. As a rule stars however bright are dimmer than the moon to planet earth.
